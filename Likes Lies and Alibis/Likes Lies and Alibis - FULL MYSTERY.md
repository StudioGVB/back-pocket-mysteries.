# PLOT

Welcome back to **The House** — the influencer collab mansion that broke the internet and almost broke everyone in it. The docuseries ended six months ago, but the drama never stopped.

Now the original housemates are reuniting for a one-off wrap party special… but things don't exactly go to plan.

Old feuds resurface. Leaked DMs come to light. And before the night is over, someone won't make it to the final post.

---

# OUTLINE

### AIM:
Your job is to uncover who planned the murder, who helped, and what the real motive was, and how the murder was committed.

### ROUNDS:
The night unfolds over 4 rounds. Each round reveals new evidence, intensifies the drama, and brings you closer to the truth.

### CLUES:
Some clues are real. Some are fake. Some are just messy. It's up to you to decide what's legit. Clues can be: DM Screenshots, Group Chats, Voice Memos, Photos, Analytics Reports, Notes App Entries, or Deleted Files.

# PREPARATION

## Prior To The Night

**ASSIGN CHARACTERS:**
Make sure each guest knows who they're playing, has time to read their bio and get their outfit ready!

**PRINT/PREPARE CLUES:**
Have all clues/character sheets/reveal printed or ready to be shared digitally. Organise by round (1–4).

**DECIDE YOUR HOST ROLE:**
Decide if you want to join the drama or help guide it — either way, the game runs itself with clues revealed round by round and additional prompts if needed.

**PREP CONFESSIONAL SETUP:** *(OPTIONAL)*
Choose a phone or camera for guests to film their confessionals. Make sure it has enough storage.

**SET THE SCENE:** *(OPTIONAL)*
Optional but iconic — print signs, set up a ring light, play trending audio in the background, scatter fake brand deal contracts around. Aesthetic is everything.

---

## On The Night

**WELCOME GUESTS:**
Remind them to stay in character and commit to the drama. It's okay to bend the truth. In fact, it's encouraged.

**KICK OFF ROUND 1:**
Read the intro aloud or play it as a cold open. Reveal the first round of clues.

**RUN THROUGH EACH ROUND:**
- Drop clues
- Give players time to theorise and stir chaos
- End with confessionals — 30s max per person per round

**REVEAL THE TRUTH:**
Who planned the murder? Who switched the drinks? Who knew, and who said nothing?

**WATCH BACK THE CONFESSIONALS:** *(OPTIONAL)*
After the reveal, replay all the drama in full. Laugh. Cry. Unfollow someone.

# HOW TO PLAY

## Rounds

**This game has 4 rounds | Rounds should last between 30–45 mins**

### START OF ROUND:
- Host reveals that round's clue drop (2–8 clues)
- Players read clues together or separately — up to you
- No one knows which clues are real, fake, or just messy

### DURING THE ROUND:
- Players stay in character
- They can interrogate, gossip, make alliances, lie, or accuse
- Encourage chaos — but also remind people to listen and gather intel

### END OF THE ROUND:
Everyone films a short confessional answering: Who do you think is guilty? Why? Who's acting shady?

**OR**

Have a big group discussion on: Who do you think is guilty? Why? Who's acting shady?

---

## Confessionals *(Optional But Iconic)*

### WHAT IS IT?
At the end of every round, every guest will record a short individual confessional video — just like a YouTube storytime.

### WHAT TO SAY:
- Who do you think is guilty?
- What theories have you developed?
- This is your chance to call someone out or have a cheeky gossip

### HOW TO RECORD?
Use one designated phone or camera. Film in a private space. Make sure each confession is less than 30 seconds long.

> **Try to stay in character whilst recording!**

### THE REVEAL:
All the confessionals are played back right before the final reveal. Either quickly edit them all together or swipe video by video. Either way will be a blast.

Kai dies. Luna believes this makes her part of his story forever — a final act of twisted devotion. The ultimate collab.
